What We Offer

Karibu Sana — You Are Welcome

TanBC provides resources and support to help Tanzanians thrive in British Columbia.

Family Network

Connect with other Tanzanian families. Arrange playdates, share resources, and help children learn Swahili and cultural traditions.

  • Family events & activities
  • Children's cultural programs
  • Parent support groups

Consular Services

Get guidance on passport renewals, visa applications, and official documentation through our liaison with the High Commission.

  • Passport assistance
  • Document notarization
  • High Commission liaison

  • Career networking

Our Story

Growing stronger together since 2011

TanBC was founded by a small group of Tanzanian families who wanted to preserve their heritage while building new lives in Canada. Today, we're a thriving community of over 540 members across British Columbia.

Our mission is simple: to provide a supportive network where Tanzanians can connect, celebrate their culture, and help each other succeed. From Uhuru Day celebrations to professional networking events, we create opportunities for meaningful connection.

"Pamoja Tunajenga"
